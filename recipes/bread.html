Bread

Description

Bread.

Ingredients

  • yeast
  • flour
  • honey
  • oil
  • water
  • salt

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, mix yeast and water with a pinch of honey
  2. Add more honey with three cups of flour and a pinch of salt
  3. Knead the dough until it is smooth and elastic
  4. Grease the bowl with oil, place dough inside, and cover with plastic wrap
  5. Allow it to rise until it double in size, and then punch all the air bubbles out
  6. Seperate the dough in to two even size balls, and then seperate in to logs in loaf pans
  7. Spray cooking spray on platic wrap and lay over pans
  8. Allow each to rise again for one hour
  9. Preheat oven to 350F and bake for 30 minutes
